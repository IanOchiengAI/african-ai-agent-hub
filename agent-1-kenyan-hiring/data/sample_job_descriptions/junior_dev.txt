JOB TITLE: Junior Software Developer
COMPANY: Nairobi Fintech Solutions Ltd
LOCATION: Nairobi (Hybrid — 3 days office, 2 days remote)

--------------

- SQL / databases (MySQL or PostgreSQL)
- Git / version control

PREFERRED SKILLS (nice to have)
-------------------------------

- FastAPI or Django
- Experience with financial systems

EXPERIENCE REQUIRED
-------------------
Minimum 1 year of professional software development experience.
We welcome candidates from non-traditional backgrounds — bootcamp graduates,
self-taught developers, and those with informal sector experience are
encouraged to apply.

EDUCATION
---------
Degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or equivalent.
Equivalent experience (bootcamp, self-taught with portfolio) is accepted.

WHAT WE OFFER
